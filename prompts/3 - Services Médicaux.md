Medical Service Dashboard – Features to Develop

Access: role: medical_service · Base route: /medical-service-dashboard

1. Overview · /medical-service-dashboard

KPI cards: active patients, today's appointments, team size, revenue

11-tile quick-action grid

Recent activity feed

2. Patients · /medical-service-dashboard/patients

Searchable patient list with hospitalization status

Add, edit, and delete patient actions

3. Team · /medical-service-dashboard/team

Team member grid: nurses, aides, therapists

Shows status, patient count, and contact info per member

Detail and add member actions

4. Schedule · /medical-service-dashboard/schedule

Planned visit list with staff assignment, date, time, and duration

Modify and cancel actions per visit

Daily and weekly stats summary

Normal / Warning / Critical classification with alerts

Add new equipment
